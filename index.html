Welcome

Welcome to the Raven Order.

The Raven Order is a secular order of modern knighthood committed to cultivating honorable individuals who strive to live lives of courage, integrity, discipline, compassion, and service. This site serves as an introduction to the Order's Founding Charter, Constitution, Member Handbook, foundational tenets, and guiding cause.
